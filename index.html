About Auntie Kate Foundation

Empowering Women, Transforming Lives Across Kenya

Catherine "Kate" Kamau

Founder & Visionary Leader

Catherine "Kate" Kamau, popularly known as Kate Actress, is a celebrated Kenyan actress, media personality, and passionate advocate for women's empowerment. With a successful career in the entertainment industry, Kate has used her platform to champion the rights and welfare of vulnerable women and girls across Kenya.

Driven by her personal experiences and witnessing the challenges faced by women in her community, Kate established the Auntie Kate Foundation to create sustainable solutions that address gender inequality, limited access to education, healthcare disparities, and economic empowerment for women.

Kate's leadership philosophy is built on the principle of "Umoja" - unity and collective action. She believes that when women support each other and are given the right opportunities, they become powerful agents of change in their families and communities.

Under Kate's visionary leadership, the foundation has grown from a personal initiative to a respected organization making significant impacts in women's education, healthcare access, economic empowerment, and advocacy across Kenya.

"We don't just provide resources; we build capacity. We don't just give opportunities; we create sustainable pathways. True transformation happens when women discover their own power to create change." - Catherine "Kate" Kamau

Our Story

The Auntie Kate Foundation was born from Catherine "Kate" Kamau's personal commitment to giving back to society, particularly focusing on the challenges faced by women and girls in Kenya. Witnessing the barriers to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities for women ignited her passion for creating meaningful change.

What started as personal philanthropic initiatives has grown into a comprehensive women's empowerment organization. Today, we work across multiple sectors, touching thousands of lives through our education support programs, healthcare initiatives, economic empowerment activities, and advocacy work.

Our journey continues as we expand our reach and deepen our impact, always guided by our commitment to empowering women and girls, one life at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donating to our foundation

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, the Auntie Kate Foundation is a registered nonprofit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible. You will receive a receipt for your records.

How will my donation be used?

93% of your donation goes directly to program services supporting women and girls. Only 7% covers administrative and fundraising costs, ensuring maximum impact.

Can I get updates on how my donation is used?

Yes! We send regular impact reports to our donors showing the specific women's programs and communities benefiting from their support.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.
